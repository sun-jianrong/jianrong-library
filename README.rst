Professor Sun Jianrong holds a Ph.D. in Higher Education Administration from Ohio
University, USA, and currently serves as Adjunct Professor at Peking University's
Graduate School of Education. He previously held positions as Associate Vice
President of Macau University of Science and Technology, Director of the
Educational Development Centre at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and
tenured Professor at the University of Rio Grande, USA. His research focuses on
quality assurance in higher education, institutional accreditation, assessment
of student learning outcomes, and applied linguistics. As an evaluator for the
Higher Learning Commission (HLC) and the Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of
Academic & Vocational Qualifications (HKCAAVQ), he has participated in
accreditation processes for dozens of institutions across China, the United
States, Hong Kong, and Macau, bringing extensive international expertise in
higher education quality management.

*This is Professor Sun Jianrong's personal digital library, featuring articles related to linguistics research.*
